Panoramic Media Wall

The landmark of Korea Tourism, the first impression of HiKR, a large media wall that expresses the space brand and identity.
K-pop Ground
Multimedia K-pop Experience Zone
A playground where global K-pop fans can experience various K-pop concepts and create their own contents through XR technology such as live studios and music video stages.
HiKR Street
An Immersive K-Culture Exhibition and Experience Zone With the Theme of "Daily(life)+(va)cation"
Enjoy permanent and special exhibitions that display Korea's cultural contents and daily life in alleyways using state-of-the-art technologies such as AI, AR, and XR.
※ Dailycation
A tourism trend (Daily+Vacation) that allows visitors to experience the everyday life of people in Korea as a form of travel.
North Wall (2022), Do Ho Suh
